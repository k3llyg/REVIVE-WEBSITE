About [REVIVE]

REVIVE is a hands-on upcycling initiative that turns unused smartphones into functional, personalized tools right in your local makerspace. Born out of a desire to resist throwaway culture, REVIVE blends DIY education with sustainability. Our kits and tutorials are made for beginners - whether you're learning to 3D print for the first time or exploring new ways to reuse old tech.
